# BookSprint — Design System

## Visual Direction
Dark editorial marketplace. Think high-end digital product store — commanding, professional, conversion-focused.

## Colors
- **Background:** `#0e0e0e` (near black)
- **Surface:** `#161616` (card/panel bg)
- **Surface 2:** `#1e1e1e` (elevated panels)
- **Accent:** `#e85d26` (burnt orange — CTA, highlights)
- **Accent Hover:** `#c94d1e`
- **Accent Muted:** `#e85d2620`
- **Text Primary:** `#f5f0eb` (warm white)
- **Text Secondary:** `#a09890` (muted warm gray)
- **Border:** `#2a2a2a`

## Typography
- **Display font:** `Bebas Neue` — for hero headings, large titles
- **Body font:** `DM Sans` — clean, modern, readable
- **Mono:** `JetBrains Mono` — for code/stats
- Hierarchy: giant display → bold section titles → regular body → small muted

## Layout
- Full-width dark sections with strong visual breaks
- Asymmetric hero with large type + floating image
- Grid-breaking accents — diagonal dividers, offset elements
- Generous padding on desktop, tighter on mobile

## Components
- Buttons: sharp edges (radius 2px), accent bg on primary, ghost outline on secondary
- Cards: `#161616` bg, `#2a2a2a` border, subtle hover glow with orange
- Inputs: dark bg `#1a1a1a`, `#2a2a2a` border, orange focus ring
- Progress steps: numbered with orange active state

## Aesthetic Keywords
Dark, editorial, bold, conversion-focused, digital product marketplace
